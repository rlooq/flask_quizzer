Help for Quizzers!

Register, Login and Profile

In order to use the quizzes on this site, you need to register first. You only have to provide us with your name and a valid email. Your information won't be shared with anyone. No spam from me either, except the occasional announcement when something big changes.


After that, you will be redirected to the Login page. Just enter your username and password, and you're in.


From that point forward, you can use those credentials to access the quizzes and your scores, which will be saved on your profile page. To access your profile page, click on the red button that shows your name on the left of navigation bar (after you log in). Only you have have access to your profile page. Well, technically I could peek at it too, it's a pain in the neck to get there, so don't worry. It's only your scores, anyway, nothing to be ashamed about. In the future it would be nice to have a general scoreboard open to everyone, to make it more competitive, but we're not there yet.


Take a Quiz

It's simple. Click on the 'Take a Quiz' option on the navigatoin bar. This will show you a drop-down menu to choose a quiz. Once selected, click on the Submit button and your quiz will appear.


Select the correct option, click the Submit button at the end of the quiz, and you'll be shown your results. That's it!


Latest News

This web app is a personal project, still in early stages, with very limited functionality. I keep working on it whenever I can by adding new features and new quizzes.


If you want to stay up to date with the latest developments, be sure to check the News section. You can find a link on the navigation bar.
