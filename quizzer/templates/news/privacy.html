Legal Notice and Privacy Policy

Quizzers! is the beta version of a very simple application used only for educational purposes. It has no affiliations with any business or for-profit institution. As such, it is provided free of charge, with no warranties of any kind. The service may be discontinued at any time without any obligations on our part regarding the quiz data stored up to that point. If that comes to pass, any information you have provided us with will also be deleted.


We don't ask you for personal information unless it is truly needed to provide this free service, that is, to let you keep track of your results when taking quizzes. The only personal information we collect is your email address, exclusively for notification purposes. You are free to provide first and last name as well if you wish, but neither of them is required. We don't store any other personal information apart from the results of the quizzes you take within this application.


We don't share your information with anyone and none of it is visible to the public.

As is true of most websites, certain information is gathered automatically by the server. This information may include Internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, the files viewed on our site (e.g. HTML pages, graphics, etc.), operating system, or date/time stamp data to analyze trends in the aggregate.


We will retain your email address and quiz results for as long as your account and/or the service is active. If you wish to terminate your account or request that we no longer use your information, please contact us at rlooq@yahoo.com.


Upon request, we will also provide you with information about whether we hold your email, name or quiz results. You may access, correct, or request deletion of such information by logging into your account or by contacting us at rlooq@yahoo.com. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe.


Authentication

We require authentication for all quiz management and data access features. Pages intended to be public are served over an SSL encrypted connection but do not require account authentication. We do not enforce password complexity requirements but recommend the following: passwords should be a minimum of 8 characters and include a mix of uppercase, lowercase and symbols and numbers. Passwords are hashed and stored in a secure database. No plain text passwords are stored. We encourage users to periodically update passwords and never use a password shared with another website.


Session Management

Each time a user is authenticated and logs in to this site, a unique session identifier is created which allows us to provide the quiz completion and correction service. This identifier doesn't track any other information.
