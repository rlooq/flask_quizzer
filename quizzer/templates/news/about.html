About Quizzers!

Quizzers! is a little pet project of mine. Who's that? An English teacher on week days and a learner of Python at nights and weekends.


Why am I doing this? While Google Forms can be quite useful for quizzes, they're rather cumbersome to create and difficult to reuse at times. Plus you have very little control over their layout and behaviour.


For a while now I've been thinking of putting together a Flask app to make it easier, for me at least. So here we are.


I don't have much time to deal with the complexities of CSS, so I turned to Bulma for help with the styling of this web site. Nothing fancy as you can see, but enough to do the job.


So far, the quizzes include multiple choice questions. You can test yourself and keep track of your scores. But hopefully, more will be added in due time. Please, report any issues you may find at this stage, your help is much appreciated.


The goal is to fine-tune the app and add more variety of questions at some point in the future, while keeping it user friendly. Needless to say, I have a day job and a family which require much attention, so I have to rely on the little time left after that.


What else? Sometimes I see dead parrots. Lovely plumage, though.
